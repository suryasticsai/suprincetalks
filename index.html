🐟 From Uncertainty to Clarity

We started this PI as a new team — with diverse roles across Dev, QA, and DE — and all the natural challenges that come with a fresh start. But through collaboration, we fished for context, mapped dependencies, ROAMed our risks, and scored our PI Objectives. We didn't just get through it — we got aligned.

📊 The State of the Team

Our feedback polls showed a mix of "totally new" and "not that good" feelings going in — perfectly normal for a new team. But look at what we achieved together:

Resolved our critical dependencies

➡️ What's Next?

We're now moving into execution. If you have questions, ask them early. If you find a dependency, highlight it immediately. The 8-point capacity is our baseline — if you need a buffer, speak up. Most importantly, take ownership of your features and finish strong.
